About the App

Your Smart Ration Shopping Assistant

E-Rashan helps you plan your monthly ration, manage your grocery budget, and share your list with the shopkeeper via QR code. Smart, simple, and offline.

🛒

100+ Pre-loaded Items

100+ grocery items across 8 categories — Grains, Pulses, Spices, Kitchen, Dairy, Beverages, Snacks & more.

🗣️

Hindi Voice Commands

Just say "उड़द दाल" and it's added! Full voice input support in Hindi language.

📴

Works Offline — 100% Free

No internet, no login, no subscription. Your data stays on your device, always.

📱

QR Code for Shopkeeper

Generate a QR code — show to your shopkeeper so they scan and see your full order instantly.

Features

Everything to Shop Smarter

From creating your first list to tracking your yearly grocery spend — E-Rashan covers it all.

🛒

Smart Grocery List

Choose from 100+ pre-loaded items across 8 categories — Grains (अनाज), Pulses (दालें), Spices (मसाले), Kitchen (रसोई), Dairy, Beverages, Snacks & more. Add quantity and price to auto-calculate totals.

🎤

Voice Input (Hindi)

Speak in Hindi — say "2 किलो चावल" and it's added instantly. Hands-free grocery planning for the whole family.

📱

QR Code Sharing

Generate a QR code from your saved order. Show it to your kirana shopkeeper — they scan and see your full list instantly.

📸

OCR Paper Scan

Have a handwritten ration list? Take a photo and our OCR auto-detects and imports items into your digital list.

💰

Budget Tracker

Set a monthly grocery budget and track spending in real-time. Visual progress bar shows how much is left — stay in control.

📊

Reports & History

Full order history, monthly spending trends, and annual reports. Re-order any past list with a single tap.

How It Works

Ready in 4 Simple Steps

From opening the app to handing your order to the shopkeeper — less than 2 minutes.

1

Open & Create List

Tap "नई लिस्ट बनाएं" on the home screen to start a fresh grocery list.

2

Select Items & Prices

Browse categories or search/speak items. Enter quantity and price per unit — totals auto-calculate.

3

Save or Share

Save to history, share via WhatsApp, or generate a QR for your shopkeeper to scan.

4

Track & Report

View monthly spending, compare trends, set budgets, and repeat orders with one tap.
